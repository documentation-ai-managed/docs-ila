## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info">

Before you begin, ensure you have:
- A modern web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
- An email address for account verification
- Optional: A GitHub account for advanced integrations

</Callout>

## Create Your ILA Account

Follow these steps to sign up and verify your account.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Dashboard" icon="monitor">
    Navigate to `https://dashboard.example.com` and click **Sign Up**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Details" icon="user">
    Provide your email, full name, and a strong password. Accept the terms of service.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification email from ILA. Click the link to activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Return to the dashboard and sign in with your credentials. You'll land on the spaces overview.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Set Up Your First Space

Spaces organize your project documentation. Create one now.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create New Space" icon="plus">
    Click **New Space** in the top-right corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name Your Space" icon="edit-3">
    Enter a name like `My Project Docs` and select a visibility (public or private).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize Settings" icon="settings">
    Add a description, set permissions, and choose a brand color like `#3B82F6`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and Enter" icon="arrow-right">
    Save the space. You'll see the empty editor interface ready for content.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Add Initial Documents

Populate your space with your first pages using these methods.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Markdown Editor" icon="edit">
    Click **New Page** and write directly.

    ````markdown
    # Welcome to My Project

    This is your first document.

    ## Features

    - Real-time collaboration
    - MDX support
    ````

  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Import from GitHub" icon="github">
    Connect your repo.

    1. Go to **Integrations** > **GitHub**.
    2. Authorize ILA.
    3. Select repo and branch to sync docs automatically.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Upload Files" icon="upload">
    Drag and drop `.mdx` or `.md` files into the space root.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Start with 3-5 pages: `introduction.mdx`, `quickstart.mdx`, `api.mdx`.
</Callout>
