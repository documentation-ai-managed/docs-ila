## Overview

Group your docs for easier navigation in ILA.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create sections">
    From your space, click **Add Section**. Name it after a project area.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add pages">
    Inside each section, click **New Page** to document topics.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Rearrange structure">
    Drag and drop sections or pages to rearrange as needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>
