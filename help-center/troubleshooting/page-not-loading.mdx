---
title: Page Not Loading
description: How to fix load issues in ILA.
---

## Symptoms

- Dashboard or docs page doesn't load
- Blank screen or loading spinner

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Refresh your browser">
    Press `Ctrl+R` (or `Cmd+R` on Mac).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear browser cache">
    Settings → Clear browsing data → Cached images/files
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your internet connection">
    Confirm WiFi and try reloading.
  </Step>
</Steps>
